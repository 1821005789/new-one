Enterprises depend on their customers, so they should understand the current and future needs of customers, meet their requirements and strive to exceed their expectations. Nowadays, the quality management system widely implemented in the world can help enterprises improve customer satisfaction. For example, customers require products to meet their needs and expectations. In any case, the acceptability of products is finally determined by customers. However, customers' needs and expectations are constantly changing at any time. Customers' satisfaction at that time does not mean that they will all be satisfied later. If customers ask for satisfaction, enterprises will already be in a passive position, and there will inevitably be neglected aspects. To take the initiative, enterprises must know the changing customer needs and expectations through regular and irregular customer surveys, and constantly improve products and the process of providing products, so as to be truly customer-centered.
